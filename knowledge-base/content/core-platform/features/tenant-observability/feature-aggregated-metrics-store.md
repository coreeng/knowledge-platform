## Motivation
No existing aggregated metrics store available for exporting.

Existing aggregated metrics store not sufficiently performant or resilient.

## Requirements
Platform providing an aggregated metrics store.

No existing aggregated metrics store to export to or it is not highly enough available.

## Additional Information
This can vary from the platform team running dedicated metrics databases  stores to documenting how tenants can deploy their own stack e.g. prometheus.

Which is picked is based on the capacity of the platform team and the tenants.
